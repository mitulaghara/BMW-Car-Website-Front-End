Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a test drive?

You can schedule a test drive by calling our sales department, using the contact form above, or visiting any of our dealership locations. We recommend booking in advance for specific M models.

What is the warranty on M Series vehicles?

All BMW M Series vehicles come with a 4-year/50,000-mile warranty. Additional extended warranty options and maintenance packages are available.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer various financing options including lease programs, purchase financing, and BMW Select Balloon Financing. Our financial specialists can help you find the best option.

What maintenance services are included?

BMW M Series includes BMW Ultimate Care with scheduled maintenance for 3 years/36,000 miles. Additional service packages are available for extended coverage.

Can I customize my M Series vehicle?

Absolutely! We offer extensive customization options through BMW Individual, including exclusive paint colors, interior trims, and performance enhancements.

What is the delivery time for new orders?

Delivery times vary based on model and customization. Standard models typically deliver in 4-8 weeks, while highly customized vehicles may take 12-16 weeks.
